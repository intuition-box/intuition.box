## Meet Reuben O. Christopher

Reuben, known as **RChris** on X, is the Co-Founder and CEO of Nexura and an Intuition Ambassador.

He has been involved in crypto since 2018 and active in Web3 since 2019, spending years learning, contributing, and exploring different corners of the space.

His building journey began with OracleLend, a lending platform built during the Intuition Testnet Builders Epoch. The lessons from that project led directly to Nexura, which the team started building in August 2025 and launched on Intuition Mainnet.

As a founder, Reuben stays close to the community — listening to feedback and understanding what people actually need.

## Meet Onuigbo Emmanuel

Onuigbo, known as **Nuel** on X, is the Co-Founder and CMO of Nexura.

He started as a content creator and community member, but a deep belief in Intuition's vision pushed him beyond content and into building.

## Nexura

Nexura is the engagement engine for the Intuition ecosystem — built to make participation more engaging, rewarding, and accessible.

Instead of asking users to navigate multiple platforms to find opportunities, Nexura brings them into one place:

- Ecosystem applications
- Quests and campaigns
- Daily engagement systems
- Rewards
- Community-driven activities

For builders, it provides a structured way to onboard users, run campaigns and lessons (coming soon), gather feedback, and drive adoption — turning a live product into an active, growing community.

Nexura is live on Intuition Mainnet today.

---

## The Problem

Building a great product is only part of growing an ecosystem — a gap both founders ran into with OracleLend, from two different angles.

Nuel kept hitting a discovery problem: talented builders were shipping real products, but users had no easy way to find them. Reuben ran into the other side of it — getting something live was one thing, but keeping users active proved far harder, exposing clear gaps in onboarding, engagement, and retention.

Discovery and engagement are two halves of the same problem. Without a connective layer between builders and users, good products stay invisible and early momentum fades.

---

## The Solution

Nexura bridges builders and users by creating a shared layer for discovery, participation, and growth.

For users, it surfaces meaningful applications and rewards consistent contribution. For builders, it offers tools to attract, educate, retain, and reward participation across the ecosystem.

The goal is a more connected Intuition — where users discover what matters, builders reach the right audience, and projects build stronger feedback loops. Nexura aims to be the central nexus that ties it all together.

## Building with Intuition

Neither founder considered themselves a builder before Intuition.

Reuben first came across the protocol in 2023 through a post by Mzcat — at the time, mostly hunting airdrops. He minted the Relics NFT in late 2024 but stayed on the sidelines until he finally dug into the docs in early 2025. The idea of a world of verified information was what won him over: once you understand the problem Intuition is solving, it's hard not to see how important it is.

Nuel discovered Intuition during the Kaito era, coming across it while exploring the pre-TGE leaderboard. Tired of spreading his attention across ecosystems, he decided to go deep on one project he genuinely believed in — and after researching the protocol, curiosity turned into conviction. Along the way he met Reuben, whose encouragement gave him the confidence to commit fully.

The turning point came during the Builders Epoch, when Billy challenged the community to build on the Intuition testnet. Reuben and Nuel teamed up, built OracleLend with Replit Agent, and — with help from Zet — deployed it live on the Testnet. Watching it go live was the moment they realized building wasn't reserved for a select few.

Around the same time, Billy and Matt shared their vision of onboarding one million builders to Intuition. Both founders landed on the same resolution independently: if there were going to be a million builders, they would be among them. Rather than wait for someone else to close the discovery gap they had felt firsthand, they turned the observation into a mission — and with encouragement from fvngbill, Nexura was born.

---

## Lessons Learned

For Reuben, the biggest lesson has been that shipping the product is only half the battle. Listening to users, understanding their frustrations, and adapting quickly matters more than features alone — many of Nexura's most impactful improvements came directly from community feedback.

For Nuel, it's that you don't have to build alone. Collaboration produces better results than working in isolation, and Intuition's culture of support — experienced developers reachable through dedicated Discord channels — makes it far easier to keep building with confidence.

Their advice to future builders converges on the same idea: don't wait until everything is perfect. Start small, solve a real problem, stay close to your users, and play the long game — building because the problem matters, not because a grant is attached to it.

## What's Next?

Nexura continues to expand its quests, campaigns, and engagement systems, with builder-facing lessons and deeper community tools on the way.

As the Intuition ecosystem grows, Nexura aims to become the central nexus for discovery and participation — the place where users find what matters and builders reach the communities that will carry their products forward.

And as the team puts it: they're just getting started.
