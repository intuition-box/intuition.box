## Trust Name Service

Trust Name Service (TNS) is a decentralized naming system for Intuition.

Just as ENS transformed Ethereum addresses into memorable names, TNS enables users to register and resolve human-readable .trust domains.

Instead of sharing long wallet addresses, users can interact through identities that are easier to remember, verify, and recognize.

---

## The Problem

Blockchain addresses were designed for machines.

Humans were never supposed to remember them.

Long strings of characters introduce friction into nearly every user experience, from payments to identity verification.

For a growing ecosystem, discoverable and human-friendly identity becomes increasingly important.

---

## The Solution

Trust Name Service introduces a native naming layer for Intuition.

Users can register .trust domains and associate them with wallets, profiles, applications, and other resources.

Benefits include:

- Human-readable identities
- Improved user experience
- Easier wallet discovery
- Better ecosystem branding
- Native SDK integrations

By making identity easier to understand, TNS helps reduce friction throughout the ecosystem.

Samoris was attracted to Intuition because of its vision around trust and identity.

## Lessons Learned

The most important lesson has been understanding that trust is not simply a social concept.

Trust can be modeled.

Trust can be structured.

Trust can become part of the infrastructure itself.

That realization fundamentally changed how Samoris thinks about identity systems.
