## What is Intuition Box?

Intuition Box is a DAO that coordinates builders contributing to the Intuition ecosystem. It funds work, allocates resources, and governs decisions through a reputation-based system.

---

## Is Intuition Box a company?

No. The DAO itself is not a legal entity.

However, **Intuition Box LLC** exists as an operational entity that can sign contracts and execute payments when required. It operates alongside the DAO, not above it.

---

## What is the Intuition Foundation?

The Intuition Foundation is the legal and fiduciary layer that supports the ecosystem.

It:
- provides monthly funding
- acts as a legal interface when needed
- ensures continuity and compliance

---

## How does governance work?

Governance is:
- reputation-based (iBOX token)
- merit-driven
- via contributions

Decisions are made through proposals and weighted signaling by active contributors.

---

## What is iBOX?

iBOX is a **non-transferable reputation unit** used for governance.

It:
- reflects contribution and participation
- determines governance influence
- decays over time with inactivity

It is **not a token for investment** and does not represent equity.

---

## How do I become a member?

You become a member by contributing to the Intuition ecosystem.

This can include:
- building software
- doing research
- writing documentation
- contributing to community or operations

Membership is based on **proof of work**, not application alone.

---

## How do grants work?

Grants fund work across the ecosystem.

They can be:
- upfront
- milestone-based
- retroactive

Grants are proposed, reviewed, and approved through governance, and executed through the treasury.

---

## Are KPIs required?

Most of the times, but not always.

The system prioritizes **execution and observable impact** over rigid KPI frameworks. Some grants may include milestones, but the system is intentionally flexible.

---

## Do I need to use the Intuition Box GitHub?

No, but it is recommended.

Using the shared GitHub organization helps ensure:
- continuity of work
- ecosystem visibility and technical support
- long-term maintainability
- free subdomain and hosting with automatic deploys

---

## What happens if my wallet is compromised?

The DAO has a formal recovery process.

- Payments are paused
- A verification process is initiated
- A new wallet can be assigned through governance

This is handled through a **Security Override Proposal** and recorded in the registry.

---

## Can I change my wallet address?

Yes, but not informally.

Wallet changes are treated as **secure state transitions** and require:
- identity verification
- governance approval
- registry update

This ensures fund safety and auditability.

---

## What is the registry?

The registry is the system of record for the DAO.

It tracks:
- grants
- wallet changes
- proposals
- security events

All important actions are logged to ensure transparency and auditability.

---

## What happens if someone stops contributing?

Nothing punitive happens, but:
- their reputation (iBOX) decays
- their governance influence decreases

They can regain influence by contributing again.

---

## Is this system decentralized?

It is **progressively decentralized**.

- Governance is already DAO-driven
- Execution still uses operational and legal layers (LLC / Foundation)

Over time, more control shifts to the DAO.

---

## Who controls the funds?

Funds are:
- provided by the Intuition Foundation
- held in a multisig Safe
- allocated by DAO governance

No single party has unilateral control.

---

## Can the DAO override decisions in emergencies?

Yes.

In critical situations:
- emergency proposals can be used
- funds can be paused
- temporary coordination mechanisms may be activated

All emergency actions must be reviewed after execution.

---

## Is this financial advice or an investment?

No.

Intuition Box is a coordination and funding system for builders.

- iBOX is not an investment
- grants are not financial products
- participation is contribution-based

---

## Where do I start?

Start by:
- contributing to the ecosystem
- engaging with the community
- submitting a proposal or grant

If you are building something meaningful, the DAO is designed to support you.
